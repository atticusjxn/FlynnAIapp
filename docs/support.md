## Common requests
- **Can’t sign in?** Use the credentials provided by your admin. If you need a reset, email us and include your organization name.
- **Call/concierge issues?** Note the time of the call, the caller number, and what should have happened. We can review the intake and summary.
- **App crashes or won’t load?** Force close and reopen the app. If it persists, share your device model, iOS version, and steps to reproduce.

## Response times
We aim to respond within one business day for new tickets and provide updates on open issues every 48 hours.
