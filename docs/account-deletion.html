Account Deletion

How to delete your FlynnAI account and data

Method 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

  1. Open the FlynnAI app on your device
  2. Navigate to Settings
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap "Account Settings"
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm your decision when prompted

Note: This is the fastest way to delete your account. All data will be removed immediately.

Method 2: Email Request

If you cannot access the app, send an email to:

Email: support@flynnai.app

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Include in your email:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your business name (if applicable)
  • Reason for deletion (optional)

We will process your request within 48 hours and send you a confirmation email.

⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All of your data including:
  • Call recordings and transcripts
  • Client information
  • Job history
  • Voicemails
  • Subscription details
will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.
