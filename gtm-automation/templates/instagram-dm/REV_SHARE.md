# IG DM Script — REV_SHARE

For: tradie influencers, business pages, industry educators with engaged audiences (5k-50k followers).

## Opener (DM 1)

```
Hey {{handle}}, atticus here — founder of Flynn AI (AI receptionist for AU tradies).

Loved your recent post about [SPECIFIC THING — read their last 3 posts before sending].

Quick ask — would you be open to a rev share? I give you a custom code (your followers get $20 off their first month, you get 30% recurring on every signup for 12 months).

Most tradies pay $29/mo, so each signup = ~$8.70/mo to you for a year. A handful of signups stacks up.

If you're keen, I'll send you the link + code today. No upfront, no contract.

Cheers,
Atticus
```

## If they reply yes

```
Sweet — here you go:

Code: {{HANDLE}}20
Link: https://flynn.ai/{{handle}} (or just App Store + apply code at checkout)
Tracking: I'll DM you a Stripe partner dashboard link tomorrow

Most successful tradie partners post:
1. A short reel showing them setting up Flynn (we can send footage)
2. A weekly story when a job comes in via Flynn ("just got booked while I was on a job")
3. A pinned link in bio

Happy to draft scripts if helpful. What's your email for partner agreement?

— Atticus
```

## Notes

- ALWAYS read their last 3 posts before opening — the personalisation line ("loved your post about X") doubles reply rate.
- 30% recurring is generous. Frame as "for 12 months" so it's not a forever liability.
- Don't pitch a flat fee — rev share aligns incentives + costs you nothing if they don't drive signups.
- If they have <5k followers, use FREE_MONTH script instead (no rev share).
