# IG DM Script — FEEDBACK

For: tradies and small business owners with <1k followers, target personas in your ICP. Goal: get the product into their hands + collect testimonials/feedback. Conversion is secondary.

## Opener

```
Hey {{handle}} — atticus, founder of Flynn AI.

I'm building an AI receptionist specifically for AU {{trade}}s — answers missed calls, texts the caller a booking link.

Talking to as many {{trade}}s as I can to make sure we're solving the right problem. Could I send you a free 2-month account in exchange for 15 mins of feedback once you've tried it?

No pitch, just genuine input on what works and what's clunky.

— Atticus
```

## If they say yes

```
Awesome, thanks {{firstName}}.

Here's a code for 2 months free: BETA-{{INITIALS}}{{NUMBER}}

App Store: https://apps.apple.com/au/app/flynnai/id6752254950

When you've used it for a week or two, hit me up — I'll grab a 15-min Zoom or just a voice message back, whatever works.

Cheers,
Atticus
```

## Why this script wins

- Reframes the ask as them helping you (lower friction than "try our product").
- 2 months free is a low CAC investment ($58) for a product feedback loop + likely testimonial.
- Often converts to paid — once they're using it daily, churn is low.
- Builds genuine relationships → testimonials → referrals.

## Notes

- Don't use this script on accounts >5k followers — REV_SHARE is more lucrative for them.
- Schedule the 15-min Zoom call as a real interview. It's worth your time.
