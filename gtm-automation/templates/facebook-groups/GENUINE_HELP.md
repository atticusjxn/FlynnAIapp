# FB Group Post — GENUINE_HELP

Offer free help / advice in your area of expertise (AI automation for tradies). Build credibility, not direct conversions.

## Template

```
G'day all — I run a small AI/automation business and have been helping AU tradies set up systems for missed calls, follow-ups, and quotes.

Happy to spend 15 min on a call with anyone here who's:
- Losing leads to missed calls
- Drowning in admin between jobs
- Trying to figure out if AI tools are worth the hype

No pitch, no sale. Just a chat about what would actually move the needle for your business.

Comment "interested" or DM me — first 5 spots this week.
```

## Variants

- **Specific topic** — "If you've ever wondered whether to add an answering service or just AI..."
- **Free audit** — "I'll do a free 5-min audit of your missed call situation if anyone wants one"

## How to use

1. Schedule the 15-min calls properly (Calendly or Google Calendar).
2. On the call: GENUINELY help. Answer questions, explain trade-offs.
3. ~70% of calls naturally end with: "what do you actually use?" — at which point Flynn comes up.
4. Don't push. Offer a trial. Move on.

## Why this is the highest-converting variant

- Builds you as a trusted advisor, not a vendor.
- Lots of qualifying happens on the call (you find out if they're a fit before you pitch).
- Even non-converts often refer mates to Flynn afterwards.

## Time investment

- 5 calls × 15 min = 1.25 hrs/week
- ~30-50% conversion to trial
- ~60% trial → paid for this segment (warmer than cold-email leads)
- Rough math: 5 calls/wk → 2-3 paid customers/month from this single channel
