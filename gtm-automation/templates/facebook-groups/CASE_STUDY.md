# FB Group Post — CASE_STUDY

Share a real (or composite, with permission) outcome. Comments naturally surface Flynn in context.

## Template

```
Spoke to a sparky in {{CITY}} this week who'd been losing 6-8 jobs a week to missed calls.

Worked out to roughly $3-4k/week in lost revenue.

He set up an AI receptionist last month — basically answers every missed call, takes a message, texts the caller a booking link.

Last week's numbers from his dashboard:
- 31 missed calls answered
- 11 turned into booked jobs
- ~$4,800 in new work that would've gone to competitors

Genuinely surprised at how well it's working. Anyone else here doing something similar? Curious to compare notes.
```

## Variants

- **Beauty/salon angle:** swap "sparky" → "salon owner", "missed jobs" → "missed bookings"
- **Plumber angle:** lean into emergency calls being highest-value
- **Office manager angle:** frame as "office manager finally fixed our missed-call problem"

## How to use

1. Post the story. Don't link Flynn in the post.
2. When a commenter asks "what receptionist?" — reply: "Mate I'll DM you, don't want to spam the group."
3. DM them the App Store link + your founder pitch.

## Notes

- ✅ Always have permission from the actual customer if you name them
- ✅ "Composite" stories are fine but the numbers should be real (don't invent stats)
- ❌ Never post the same case study in the same group twice
- 🔥 If you have screenshots of real dashboard data, even better — mods rarely flag image posts
