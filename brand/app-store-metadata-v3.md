## Description (4000 chars max)

Flynn is the keyboard that drafts your texts so they sound exactly like you — then books the moment everyone agrees.

**How it works**
Copy any message. Switch to the Flynn keyboard inside Messages (or WhatsApp, or anywhere you type). It reads what you copied and drafts three or four replies in your actual voice. Tap the one you'd send. Done.

Nothing sends on its own. You read, you pick, you send.

**Sounds like you — not a robot**
During setup you give Flynn a handful of replies you'd actually send. It locks in your tone, your slang, your punctuation habits. Then it gets sharper every time you pick a draft, learning from what you actually use.

**Knows when you're free**
Connect your Apple Calendar or Google Calendar and Flynn drafts around your real availability. Agree on a time in the conversation and it's booked straight into your calendar — no flicking between apps.

**Knows your business**
Your services, prices, hours and service area live in Flynn's Brain. Quotes and booking answers are right every time, without you having to type them out again.

**Who uses Flynn**
Tradies, freelancers, salon owners, real estate agents, side hustlers, anyone who relies on texts to get things booked. If you reply to messages to run your work, Flynn saves you time.

**Free to start**
A handful of drafts every day, one connected calendar, replies in your voice — free, no card needed. Go Pro for unlimited drafts, calendar booking from replies, full tone tuning, and your complete business brain.
